Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of volunteer work is available?

We offer both local and international service opportunities focused on inclusive education, accessibility, and community development.

Do I need prior experience to join?

No! We provide training and match you with projects that align with your interests and comfort level.

How are donations used?

Donations fund assistive tools, digital training programs, and community travel initiatives for people with disabilities.
